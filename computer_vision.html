Computer Vision

I started looking at computer vision methods as a means to collect information about "acting" from film and video sources. I ended up becoming deeply interested in the field of computer vision, particularly as it pertains to scene understanding and human pose estimation. Of course my interest doesn't stop there, as there are so many aspects to understanding people and using computer vision to do that is my aim.

You will see plenty of references to pose estimation, action recognition, object detection, person reidentification, face, hands and head tracking etc.
